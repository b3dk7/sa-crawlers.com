about us

We are a Cape Town based team that specialize in sentiment analysis, a method to understand public attitude toward a particular topic. Using artificial intelligence in combination with web crawlers (automated web browsers), we can evaluate thousands of posts and articles without having to read them manually. We design our own search engines from scratch and utilize well maintained AI solutions if needed to generate almost any type of report / intelligence.
